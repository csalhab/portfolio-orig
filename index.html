About Me

I'm not originally from the United States. I was born in Brasil, in the state of Para in a city called Belem (which translates to Bethlehem in English). This state is in Brasil's north and the city is a basin city to the Amazon River.

Fun fact: Belem is located just over a degree below the Equator.

Portuguese is my 1st language and Enlish is actually my 3rd language and that's because when I moved to the United States when I was 5 years old, there were no Portuguese speaking teachers available in the school district. So I was assigned a Spanish speaking teacher (who was brilliant and found similar words in Spanish to Portuguese and while she taught me English, she also taught me Spanish).

So when I was deciding what to study in university, I decided to study programming/coding because I enjoyed that experience in learning English.

I work hard to keep up with current technologies and look forward to my next adventure in Software Engineering.
